    • Potato Skins Recipe

    Published 18th July 2023

    Indulge in these delicious and crispy potato skins, perfect as a mouthwatering appetizer or a delightful snack. These savory treats are loaded with melted cheese, crispy bacon, and a touch of green onions, making them a crowd-pleasing favorite for any gathering or movie night. Simple to prepare and bursting with flavor, potato skins are sure to be a hit with friends and family!

    Ingredients:

    • 4 large russet potatoes
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
    • 2 green onions, finely sliced
    • Sour cream, for serving (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. Scrub the potatoes clean under running water to remove any dirt. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
    3. Using a fork, pierce the potatoes all over to allow steam to escape while baking.
    4. Rub each potato with olive oil, ensuring they are well coated.
    5. Sprinkle the potatoes with salt and black pepper, evenly coating each one.
    6. Place the potatoes directly on the prepared baking sheet and bake them in the preheated oven for about 50 to 60 minutes, or until the skins are crispy, and a fork can easily pierce through the flesh.
    7. Remove the baked potatoes from the oven and let them cool slightly until they are safe to handle.
    8. Slice the potatoes in half lengthwise, and using a spoon, carefully scoop out most of the flesh, leaving about 1/4 inch (0.6 cm) of potato flesh attached to the skins. Save the scooped-out potato for another use (e.g., mashed potatoes).
    9. Increase the oven temperature to 450째F (230째C).
    10. Arrange the hollowed-out potato skins back on the baking sheet, skin side down.
    11. Fill each potato skin with a generous amount of shredded cheddar cheese and crumbled bacon.
    12. Return the filled potato skins to the oven and bake for an additional 8 to 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    13. Once the potato skins are out of the oven, sprinkle them with sliced green onions for a fresh, flavorful touch.
    14. Serve the potato skins hot as is or with a dollop of sour cream on top for added creaminess (optional).

    Enjoy these delectable potato skins while they're warm and watch them disappear as your guests savor every scrumptious bite!
