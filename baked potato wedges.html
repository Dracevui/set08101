Baked Potato Wedges Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Wash and cut potatoes into wedges.
  3. In a large bowl, toss potato wedges with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden and crispy.
