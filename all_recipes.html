    Loaded Potato Skins

    Loaded potato skins are baked potato skins that are filled with bacon, cheese, sour cream, and chives for a delicious appetizer or snack. The potatoes are usually baked first, then the insides are scooped out and the skins are filled with the toppings before being baked again until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

    Potato Croquettes

    Potato croquettes are mashed potatoes that are mixed with cheese, herbs, and spices, then breaded and fried until golden brown. They can be shaped into balls or cylinders and are often served as an appetizer or snack.

    Hasselback Potatoes

    Hasselback potatoes are thinly sliced potatoes that are seasoned with herbs and baked until crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. The potatoes are sliced almost all the way through, but not quite, so that they stay together while baking. They can be seasoned with a variety of herbs and spices, and are often served as a side dish.
