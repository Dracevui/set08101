    • Hasselback Potatoes Recipe

    Published 19th July 2023

    Hasselback Potatoes are a mouthwatering side dish that feature crispy edges and a tender, buttery interior. Originating from Sweden, these delightful spuds are a unique take on the classic baked potato. In this recipe, we will guide you through the simple steps to create these visually impressive and utterly delicious Hasselback Potatoes.

    Ingredients:

    • 4 large russet potatoes
    • 2 tablespoons melted butter
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. Wash and scrub the potatoes thoroughly. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
    3. Place a potato between two chopsticks or wooden spoons. Make thin, evenly spaced cuts along the potato, slicing about three-quarters of the way through. The chopsticks will prevent you from cutting all the way through the potato.
    4. In a small bowl, mix melted butter, olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and dried thyme.
    5. Brush the butter mixture generously over each potato, making sure to get in between the slices.
    6. Place the potatoes on the prepared baking sheet and bake them in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes or until they are golden brown and crispy on the edges.
    7. During the last 10 minutes of baking, sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the potatoes and return them to the oven to melt the cheese.
    8. Remove the Hasselback Potatoes from the oven and let them cool slightly. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley before serving.
    9. Serve these impressive Hasselback Potatoes as a delightful side dish alongside your favorite main course, and enjoy the delightful blend of flavors and textures.

    These Hasselback Potatoes are sure to be a hit at your next meal. With their crispy edges, creamy insides, and a hint of Parmesan cheese, they are an irresistible addition to any dinner table. Try out this simple yet impressive recipe and watch your guests marvel at these delectable potatoes!
