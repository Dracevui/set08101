    • Potato Croquettes Recipe

    Published 19th July 2023

    Jump to Recipe

    Potato croquettes are a classic appetizer or side dish that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. They are easy to make and can be enjoyed by everyone. In this recipe, we will show you how to make delicious potato croquettes that are perfect for any occasion.

    Ingredients:

    • 4 large russet potatoes
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 1/4 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme (optional)
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup breadcrumbs
    • Vegetable oil, for frying

    Instructions:

    1. Start by peeling the russet potatoes and cutting them into chunks. Rinse the potatoes under cold water to remove excess starch.
    2. In a large pot, add the potato chunks and cover them with cold water. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat and cook the potatoes until they are fork-tender, usually about 15-20 minutes.
    3. Drain the cooked potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the butter, milk, grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme (if using), salt, and black pepper.
    4. Using a potato masher or a fork, mash the potatoes until smooth and creamy. Adjust the seasoning to your taste preferences.
    5. Let the mashed potatoes cool slightly before shaping the croquettes. Take a spoonful of the mashed potato mixture and form it into an oval-shaped croquette with your hands. Repeat until all the mixture is used.
    6. In a shallow dish, beat the eggs. In another shallow dish, place the all-purpose flour. In a third shallow dish, add the breadcrumbs.
    7. Coat each croquette first in flour, shaking off any excess. Dip it into the beaten eggs, allowing any excess to drip off. Finally, coat it evenly with breadcrumbs, pressing lightly to adhere.
    8. Heat vegetable oil in a deep skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat. Carefully add the coated croquettes to the hot oil and fry them in batches until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes per side.
    9. Once the croquettes are done frying, transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels to remove any excess oil.

    Serve the hot and crispy potato croquettes with your favorite dipping sauce or as a delightful side dish to complement any meal. Enjoy these heavenly treats and watch them disappear from the plate in no time!
