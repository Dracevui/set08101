    • Mashed Potato Sushi Rolls Recipe

    Published 19th July 2023

    Jump to Recipe

    Mashed Potato Sushi Rolls are a creative twist on traditional sushi, where seasoned mashed potatoes replace the traditional rice. Wrapped in nori seaweed, these rolls feature a variety of sushi fillings like avocado, cucumber, smoked salmon, or tempura vegetables. Slice them into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce or spicy mayo for an inventive and flavorful experience.

    Ingredients:

    • Mashed potatoes
    • Nori seaweed sheets
    • Assorted sushi fillings (avocado, cucumber, smoked salmon, tempura vegetables, etc.)
    • Soy sauce and/or spicy mayo, for dipping

    Instructions:

    1. Spread a thin layer of mashed potatoes on a sheet of nori seaweed.
    2. Arrange your choice of sushi fillings (avocado, cucumber, smoked salmon, tempura vegetables, etc.) along the center of the mashed potatoes.
    3. Carefully roll up the nori, using a bamboo sushi mat for precision.
    4. Slice the rolls into bite-sized pieces.
    5. Serve the Mashed Potato Sushi Rolls with soy sauce and/or spicy mayo for dipping.

    These Mashed Potato Sushi Rolls offer a unique and flavorful twist on traditional sushi. Enjoy the combination of creamy mashed potatoes with a variety of sushi fillings, and experience a delightful fusion of textures and flavors. Try out this innovative recipe and impress your guests with this creative spin on sushi!
