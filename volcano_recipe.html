    • Loaded Potato Volcanoes

    Published 19th July 2023

    Jump to Recipe

    Experience an eruption of flavors with these unique and visually stunning Loaded Potato Volcanoes. Hollowed-out small, boiled potatoes serve as the base, ready to be filled with a mouthwatering mixture of mashed potatoes, cheese, cooked bacon, and chopped green onions. Once baked to golden perfection, a fiery drizzle of spicy sriracha sauce completes this explosive dish.

    Ingredients:

    • 8 small russet potatoes
    • 1 cup mashed potatoes
    • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
    • 1/2 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
    • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
    • 1/4 cup sour cream
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
    • Sriracha sauce (optional, for drizzling)

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. Wash and scrub the potatoes thoroughly. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
    3. Place a potato between two chopsticks or wooden spoons. Make thin, evenly spaced cuts along the potato, slicing about three-quarters of the way through. The chopsticks will prevent you from cutting all the way through the potato.
    4. In a small bowl, mix melted butter, olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and dried thyme.
    5. Brush the butter mixture generously over each potato, making sure to get in between the slices.
    6. Place the potatoes on the prepared baking sheet and bake them in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes or until they are golden brown and crispy on the edges.
    7. During the last 10 minutes of baking, sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the potatoes and return them to the oven to melt the cheese.
    8. Remove the Loaded Potato Volcanoes from the oven and let them cool slightly. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley before serving.
    9. Serve these impressive Loaded Potato Volcanoes as a delightful side dish alongside your favorite main course, and enjoy the delightful blend of flavors and textures.

    These Loaded Potato Volcanoes are sure to be a hit at your next meal. With their crispy edges, creamy fillings, and a hint of spiciness, they are an irresistible addition to any dinner table. Try out this explosive recipe and watch your guests marvel at these delectable delights!
